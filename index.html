Why Studio Ghibli is good

Studio Ghibli is a Japanese studio responsible for some of the finest feature-length animation films ever produced, inspiring countless artists and filmmakers. Common themes and artistic styles show up throughout the Ghibli universes; environmentalism, flight, childhood, metamorphosis, weather, worlds within worlds, community and mythology from both Japan and Europe. Whilst every story takes places in seemingly different worlds, these common thematic threads often tie them together into something unmistakable.
